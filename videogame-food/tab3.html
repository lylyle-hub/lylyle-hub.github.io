In The Outer Worlds, consumable brands are heavily satirical, corporatized products. Items are categorized into foods, sugary drinks, caffeine, alcohol, and drugs, with brands ranging from ultra-cheap to high-end. Consumable companies are "bad" because they are ruthless satires of unchecked megacorporations. They prioritize maximum profit and monopolies over worker safety, consumer health, and basic ethics. Companies abuse animals, do testing on their employees and animals, and treat employees as disposable. Corporations have exclusive control over essential survival goods, meaning desperate colonists are forced to buy overpriced, low-quality necessities.
