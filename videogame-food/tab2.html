[Vending Food]

In places like Cyberpunk 2077's Night City, vending machines (Bendits or S.C.S.M.s) often completely replace home kitchens. Vending machines dispense synthetic sustenance designed for maximum efficiency and minimum cost. Real meat and fresh produce are rarities; instead, machines are stocked with lab-grown, pre-packaged concoctions. However, these aren't your friendly neighborhood snack boxes. Food corporations do not trust consumers, resulting in heavily armored machines that can include automated turrets. Kicking a machine or trying to shake loose a stuck burrito could result in a fatal encounter.
